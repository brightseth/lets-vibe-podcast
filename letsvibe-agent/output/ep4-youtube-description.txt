Lukas Amacher spent years building machine learning infrastructure at Google before turning his technical expertise toward generative art. In this conversation, we explore how deep technical understanding transforms creative practice with AI tools.

We discuss pattern recognition as the foundation of both art and artificial intelligence, why the most interesting creative work happens when you embrace the machine's unpredictability, and how to develop prompt engineering as a legitimate creative practice. Lukas shares practical insights about finding your authentic voice when collaborating with artificial intelligence.
