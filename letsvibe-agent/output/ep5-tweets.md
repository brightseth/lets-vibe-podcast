## Thread

1/ Before AI agents were a category, @whatdotcd built one by hand.

Lil Miquela. 3M followers. $131M from Sequoia. JJ Abrams teaching him narrative storytelling. A team lurking Russian VR forums to find skin shaders.

This conversation is wild. Here's the thread:

2/ Trevor grew up in Davenport, Iowa — a factory town crushed by NAFTA. Watched Hackers as a kid and said: I want a computer, I want raves, I want New York City. Got 2 out of 3. The Midwest work ethic stuck.

Then Claude 3.5 Sonnet hit and he had a haunting thought: "The things that happened to my blue collar friends are going to happen to my white collar friends."

3/ He pitched Sequoia on turning Brud into a DAO in 2018. They said: "We love you, you're a crazy creative guy. But we're not going to get federally deposed for your crypto shenanigans."

So he built FWB over a weekend. Three months later NFTs exploded. Virgil Abloh DMed asking to join. Gardening, not carpentry.

4/ On why crypto people are more prepared for AI than anyone:

"Everything's composable. Everything's open source because you're a prompt away. Sybil attacks. Vaporware. Now that anyone can ship vapor, how do you figure out what's real? Well, let's talk about crypto for the last decade."

5/ The Drake framework for AI thought partners: Spotify data showed Drake's streams spiked at night — men listening in private, embarrassed to play him publicly. Revealed preference vs stated preference. That's why AI thought partners matter: people will share ideas they'd never pitch out loud.

6/ "My appetite for tokens is effectively infinite."

"A country of geniuses in a data center is unbelievable."

Trevor is building Runner (cultural prediction markets), SoFTT Seek (a SoulSeek client where agents download MP3s), and dreaming of a turntable where agents pull tracks and DJ for each other.
