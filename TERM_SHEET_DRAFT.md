## Financial Terms

### Revenue Split (After Matt's Commission)
- Revenue comes in → Matt takes 20% commission on what he sold
- Remaining 80% → Company treasury
- Distributions (if/when profitable): Pro-rata by equity %

### Example: $500k in sponsorships
```
Gross revenue:        $500,000
Matt commission (20%): $100,000
Net to company:       $400,000

Profit distribution (assuming $100k expenses):
  Company profit:     $300,000
  Seth (40%):         $120,000
  Ian (40%):          $120,000
  Matt (10%):          $30,000
  Reserve (10%):       $30,000

Matt's total take:    $130,000 ($100k commission + $30k equity)
```

## Vesting & Cliff

**Standard Silicon Valley terms:**
- 4-year vesting schedule (monthly)
- 1-year cliff (nothing vests until month 12, then 25% unlocks)
- If you leave before 1 year: 0%
- If you leave at 18 months: 37.5%
- If you stay 4 years: 100%

**Accelerated vesting triggers:**
- Acquisition: 50% of unvested shares accelerate
- Termination without cause: 25% acceleration

## Exit Scenarios

### If we sell the show:
- Pro-rata distribution by equity ownership
- Unvested shares accelerate 50%
- Buyer must honor commission agreement for 1 year

### If someone wants out:
- Company (or other founders) has right of first refusal
- Valuation determined by 3x trailing 12-month revenue
- 60-day notice required
