## The Flywheel

### 1. Content → Discovery
- **Let's Vibe! podcast** features builders and their projects
- Projects get submitted to **vibecodings** directory
- Audience discovers new tools, gets inspired

### 2. Discovery → Community
- Builders from **vibecodings** connect on **/vibe**
- Collaborations form, projects evolve
- Community grows organically

### 3. Community → Commerce
- Builders optimize setups via **vibestation**
- Hardware recommendations, affiliate revenue
- Sponsors want access to this audience

### 4. Commerce → Sustainability
- Revenue flows through **Spirit Protocol**
- Token mechanics align incentives
- Creators get paid, ecosystem sustains

### 5. Sustainability → Content
- Funded creators make more content
- More content attracts more builders
- Flywheel accelerates

## Voice & Tone

**Inspirations:**
- Rick Rubin (calm curiosity)
- TED Talks (sophisticated, substantial)
- Oprah (warm, accessible)
- Acquired (deep research)

**Never say:**
- "Excited to announce"
- "Revolutionary"
- "Game-changing"
- Empty superlatives

**Always:**
- Show, don't tell
- Process over product
- Practical over theoretical
- Respect the audience
